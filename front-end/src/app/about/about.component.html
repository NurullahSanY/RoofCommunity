Roof Agriculture Community

Roof Agriculture communities are like gardens on top of buildings in cities. People work together to grow food there. They use smart ways to save water and grow plants on walls. These rooftop gardens not only give fresh food but also teach everyone to take care of the environment and build a strong community.They also help build a strong community in the city.
